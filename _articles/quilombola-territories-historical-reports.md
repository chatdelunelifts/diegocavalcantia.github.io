abstract: >
  This article analyses the role of historical expert reports (laudos históricos periciais)
  in legal proceedings concerning the demarcation of quilombola territories in Brazil.
  Drawing on the experience of co-authoring such a report for communities in Ceará, the
  article reflects on the epistemological and political dimensions of producing historical
  knowledge for legal use, and on the relationships between academic historians, local
  communities, and state institutions in processes of historical justice.
